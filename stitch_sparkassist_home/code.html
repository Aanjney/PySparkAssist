Explain the concept of 'Tonal Depth' in minimalist UI design.

bolt

Tonal depth is the practice of using subtle variations in background luminosity rather than explicit lines or borders to define hierarchy and boundaries. In SparkAssist, we utilize tiers of grey and white—like surface and surface-container-low—to create a sense of layering.

This approach minimizes visual noise, allowing the user's focus to remain entirely on the editorial content while subconsciously perceiving the structural organization of the space.
